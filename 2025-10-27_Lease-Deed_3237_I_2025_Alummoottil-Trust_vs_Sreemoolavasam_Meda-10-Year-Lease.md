## Operative clauses

**1. RECITALS – INTEGRAL PART** — "The recitals aforementioned shall form an integral part of the operative part of this Lease Deed."

**2. PERIOD OF LEASE** — "The lease is for a period of 10 years with effect from 27/10/2025 and ending with 26/10/2035."

**3. RENT** — "The monthly rent is at the rate of Rs. 60,000/- (Rupees Sixty Thousand only) per month payable on or before the fifth day of every english calendar month and pro-rata amount of rent for any broken period of less than a month."

**4. ELECTRICITY, PHONE AND WATER CONNECTIONS TO THE PROPERTY** — "All the charges for the electricity and water consumed and all other charges for any services consumed by the Lessee shall be paid by the Lessee without making the Lessor in any way liable for the same."

**5. SUB LEASE** — "The Lessee shall not have any right to sub-lease the said property to any other persons."

**6. ALTERATIONS** — "The Lessee shall not make any additions, alterations or improvements in the said Meda of a permanent nature."

**7. DEFAULT OF RENT** — "If the Lessee fails to pay the rent on or before the stipulated date then the Lessee shall be liable to pay interest to the Lessor at the rate of 12% per annum of the amount defaulted without prejudice to the other remedies of the Lessor."

*[Note: no clauses numbered 8 or 9 appear in the scan; numbering jumps from 7 to 10.]*

**10. NATURE OF USE PERMITTED** — "The Lessee shall be entitled and permitted to use the said property only for its Wellness and Leisure retreat befitting its profile and protecting its legacy and heritage purpose."

**11. TAX, CHARGES, OUTGOINGS ON THE SAID PROPERTY** — "The Lessor shall pay the property tax payable to the Panchayath with respect to the said Meda."

**12. INSPECTION** — "The Lessor shall have the right to inspect the said property at all reasonable times with prior intimation to the Lessee without causing any unreasonable obstructions to the business of the Lessee and the Lessee shall not object to the same."

**13. EXPIRY OF LEASE**
a) "On expiry of the period of this lease, by mutual understanding the period of lease may be further extended for another 10 years period by mutual consent of the Lessor and the Lessee by way of entering into a new registered Lease Deed."
b) "After the said 10 years of Lease period, the Lessee shall vacate and surrender vacant possession of the said property to the Lessor, if the period of Lease is not extended as aforesaid, without any objections whatsoever and the Lessee shall hand over the said property in the condition in which it had originally taken possession of the same, normal wear and tear exempted."

**14. TERMINATION OF LEASE**
1) "The Lessor shall have the right to terminate this Lease if the monthly lease rent is unpaid by the Lessee to the Lessor for three consecutive months. Or"
2) "If the Lessee or their Staff or their Legal Representatives or their Guests cause damage to the leased out property by way of omission or commission verified and confirmed and assessed by mutually appointed qualified Third Party within the shortest duration of time from the occurrence of damage. Or"
3) "If the Lessee causes damage by way of omission or commission to the reputation of the Lessor verified and confirmed and assessed by mutually appointed qualified Third Party within the shortest duration of time from the occurrence of damage. Or"
4) "The Lessee may terminate this lease if the Project envisaged hereunder as part of Wellness and Leisure Retreat becomes not commercially viable after providing three months prior written notice to the Lessor regarding the same."

**15. COMMUNICATIONS** — "Any communications or notices to be sent by the Parties to each other related to this Agreement shall be sent to the respective addresses of the Parties mentioned herein or to any other address notified in writing by the Parties to each other for the said purpose."

**16. DELAY** — "Any delay on the part of any of the Parties in exercising any of their respective rights under this Agreement or of any legal rights arising out of or related to such rights shall not be considered as waiver of such rights by the concerned Party/Parties unless explicitly expressed so in writing by the concerned Party/Parties."

**17. SEVERABILITY** — "If any of the terms and conditions of this Agreement are declared by any judicial or quasi Judicial body to be illegal or unenforceable, such terms and conditions shall be considered to have been severed off from this Agreement and the rest of the terms and conditions of this Agreement shall remain to be binding on the Parties, unless the contrary intention is explicitly expressed by the Parties in writing and/or unless such terms and conditions are inevitable for the performance of the obligations of the respective Parties of this Agreement to the other Party/ies."

**18. HEADINGS** — "The headings appended to clauses of this Agreement are meant only for purposes of convenience, and reference, and shall not be construed to mean or indicate anything else, and shall not be taken into account in the interpretation of these presents."

**19. DISPUTE RESOLUTION** — "In the case of any dispute or any difference between the parties arising out of or in relation to this lease including dispute or difference as to the validity of this lease deed or interpretation of any of the provisions of this agreement, the same shall be resolved by mutual discussion. If the parties fail to settle the dispute or difference mutually, then the same shall be resolved by Arbitration in accordance with and subject to the provisions of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 or any modifications or amendments thereto, and the award of such Arbitration shall be final and binding on both parties. The venue for such arbitration shall be Cochin and language of arbitration shall be English. Only courts at Ernakulam shall have jurisdiction to adjudicate over matters related to such Arbitration. The provisions of this clause shall survive the termination of this lease."

**20. EXPENSES OF LEASE DEED** — "The Lessee shall bear the stamp duty, registration fee, legal and incidental expenses for the preparation and registration of this lease deed and its duplicates hereunder mentioned exclusively."

**21. ORIGINAL AND DUPLICATE** — "This Lease Deed is prepared in Original with a Duplicate thereof. The Original of this Lease Deed shall be kept by the Lessee and the Duplicate thereof shall be kept by the Lessor."

**22. FAIR VALUE** — "The fair value for the scheduled land is Rs.13,20,000/- per Are. Thus, the value for the scheduled land is Rs. 61,31,400/- and the total rent to be paid is Rs. 72,00,000/-."

**23. STAMP DUTY** — "A Stamp duty of Rs.1,15,400/- is paid for the Original of this Agreement and a Stamp duty of Rs.500/- is paid for the duplicate of this Agreement."
