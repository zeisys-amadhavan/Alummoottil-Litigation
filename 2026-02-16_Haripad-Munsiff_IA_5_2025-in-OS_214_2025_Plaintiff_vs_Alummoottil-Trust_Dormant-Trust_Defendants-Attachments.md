**English summary (not part of the document):** The DFO, Ranni, responds to Anoop Madhavan's complaint (02.08.2025) about wild buffalo skull ("thalayottu") and antlers ("mankombu") allegedly removed unlawfully from the Alumootil Meda building. The Ranni Range Forest Officer investigated and filed a final report. The investigation did not establish that the said skull/antlers were present in the Meda. Although the complaint offered to supply documents, photos and witness statements, the Range Officer reported that attempts to contact the complainant by phone and email yielded no evidence or records. Treating it as a dispute among trust members, the DFO states that if clear, supporting evidence is furnished, the matter will be examined and re-investigated if found necessary.
