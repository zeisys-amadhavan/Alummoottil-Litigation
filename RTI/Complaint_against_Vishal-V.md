# COMPLAINT

**To**

The Hon'ble Chief Minister, Government of Kerala (through the Chief Minister's Office).

Copy to: The Director General of Police, Kerala; the State Police Complaints Authority.

**From**

Anoop Madhavan, S/o Radhakrishnan Madhavan, Alummoottil, Muttom, Pallippad, Alappuzha (also at 12 TV Road, Chetpet, Chennai – 600031).

**Subject:** Seeking inquiry and action against SI (Gr) Vishal V and the supervising officers of Kareelakulangara Police Station for the partial and improper handling of, and inaction on, my complaint dated 09.08.2024 — reg.

Respected Sir/Madam,

- On **09.08.2024** I was assaulted at my family property, Alummoottil Meda. I called 112 at 10:56 a.m.; SI (Gr) Vishal V (PEN 170080) and Dvr CPO 7029 Aneez reached the scene; I was treated the same day (right subscapularis tendon injury); and at about 4:20 p.m. I submitted a **written complaint** at Kareelakulangara P.S. naming **Sivadasan and Ramesh Chandra, who orchestrated the attack, and four (then) unidentified assailants** (Complaint No. 15281021-2024-5-00770). I re-filed the same complaint on the Thuna portal on 23.08.2024 (No. 15281021-2024-5-00825).

- The police's own RTI replies (15.12.2025; 05.02.2026) admit that, on my complaint, **the officers who attended my 112 call and the scene received, examined or prepared no official record and recorded no statement, SI Vishal V and CPO Aneez prepared no note or report on my complaint, and no attempt was made to contact me** between 09.08.2024 and 31.08.2024; and that the Station holds no General Diary entry and no 112 log for the incident. On my escalations to the District Police Chief, Alappuzha (02.09.2024, u/s 173 BNSS) and to the Deputy Inspector General of Police (17.09.2024), the Station instead e-mailed me on 21.09.2024 that the complaint stood **"closed" without investigation**. No FIR was registered on my complaint until **17.12.2024 — 130 days later — and only on a Home Ministry reference**.

- By contrast, **SI (Gr) Vishal V (PEN 170080) registered FIR No. 574/2024 against me within 58 minutes** of an oral complaint by Arun B Unni on 10.08.2024 and signed it. Arun B Unni is neither of my family nor connected to the Trust; his FIR records a Kollam address, and he is a labourer engaged through a contractor of one Sivadasan, **falsely shown as "Alummoottil Meda's Work Supervisor."**

- The partiality is further evident from the accused arrayed. In **CMP 2828/2024** I named six accused — Sivadasan and Ramesh Chandra (the orchestrators), Natarajan, Nandakumar, Arun B Unni and Stalin. In **Crime No. 956/2024** the police arrayed only Nadarajan, Nandakumar, Bobi and Stalin — **omitting the two orchestrators and Arun B Unni** (who himself filed the counter-FIR against me), and **adding one Bobi, whom I never named** and who appears in FIR 574/2024 as a co-worker of its complainant.

- The said conduct of SI (Gr) Vishal V, and the **failure of his supervising officers to correct it** despite my repeated escalations (the said complaints, the RTI applications and the CMO-portal petition No. G 2241200251), **discloses partiality, inaction and apparent suppression** of my case. The supervising officers in office at the relevant time, in ascending order of rank, are: **Sri Nizamuddin J** (Inspector & SHO, who issued the RTI replies); **Sri Babukuttan N** (Dy.S.P., Kayamkulam); **Sri Mohanachandran** (S.P., Alappuzha); **Smt S. Ajeetha Begum** (D.I.G., Trivandrum Range); and **Sri S. Syamsundar** (I.G.P., South Zone).
