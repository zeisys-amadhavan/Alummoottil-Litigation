## Affidavit

I, **Anoop Radhakrishnan Madhavan**, aged 48 years, S/o Radhakrishnan Madhavan, residing at 1201 Connecticut Ave NW Ste 531, PMB # 722189, Washington DC, USA, represented by my duly constituted Agent holding Power of Attorney, AG Kishore, aged 71 years, S/o AK Gopalan, r/o 12 TV Road, Chetpet, Chennai, Tamil Nadu — 600031 do hereby sincerely and solemnly affirm and state as follows:

### 1. Competence to swear

I am the plaintiff in the above suit and I am conversant with the facts and hence competent to swear this affidavit.

### 2. Nature of the suit

The above suit is filed for a declaration that the Plaintiff is a Beneficiary under Trust Deed No. 2/IV/2007 of the Alummoottil Tharavad Trust and for consequential reliefs including a declaration that Amendment Deeds No. 66/IV/2022 and 17/IV/2024 are illegal and void. The suit further seeks directions to restore the Plaintiff's rights over the ancestral Meda property, to restrain the Defendants from committing further acts of exclusion and to compel the production of the Trust accounts. The suit is filed based on the Plaintiff's lawful rights as a lineal descendant and Co-Beneficiary under the original Trust Deed No. 2/IV/2007 dated 04/01/2007.

### 3. Concealment of Trust records

After the Plaintiff raised objections to the non-transparency and misuse of Trust funds and immovable property, the Defendants allegedly executed fraudulent amendment deeds excluding him unlawfully. They have also concealed the original Trust Deed, its amendments and the full set of Trust financial records from 2007 to 2024. These documents are absolutely essential for the fair and complete adjudication of the present suit.

### 4. Vital deeds registered at SRO Cheppad

The original Trust Deed No. 2/IV/2007, as well as subsequent Amendment Deeds 66/IV/2022 and 17/IV/2024, are vital documents for proper adjudication of the suit. These deeds are registered in the Sub Registrar Office, Cheppad.

### 5. Ignored copy requests

The Plaintiff had requested copies of the same from SRO, Cheppad but such requests were ignored. The Defendants are deliberately suppressing the original documents and trust financial records.

### 6. Financial records in exclusive control of D2

The Plaintiff also seeks production of the financial statements, ledgers, audit records, and account books of the Alummoottil Tharavad Trust for the period from 2007 to 2024, which are in the exclusive control of the 2nd Defendant.

### 7. Prayer

It is therefore most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to direct the 1st and 2nd respondent to produce the Trust Deed No. 2/IV/2007 and the subsequent Amendment Deeds 66/IV/2022 and 17/IV/2024 and further direct the 1st and 2nd Defendant to produce the financial statements, ledgers, audit reports, and complete account records of the Alummoottil Tharavad Trust from the year 2007 to 2024, as these records are essential for the fair and effective adjudication of the present suit.

### 8. Separate petition and prejudice

A separate petition for the said purpose is filed along with and if the same is not allowed I will be put to irreparable injury and hardships.

Dated this the 28th day of July 2025

All the facts stated above are true and correct to the best of my knowledge, information and belief.
