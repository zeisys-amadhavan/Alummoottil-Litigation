**Extract of Resolution taken on the Alummoottil Tharavad Trust meeting held on 22.12.2023**

1. "Resolved that the trustees of Alummoottil Tharavad Trust means the Authors and other trustees (5 Trustees) who are the present owners of the Alummoottil Tharavad Properties. They are the following members-

   1 Ramesh chandran Channar alias Ramesh chandran M  
   2 Radhakrishnan Channar alias Radhakrishnan M  
   3 Udayabhanu Channar alias Udayabhanu M  
   4 Sivadasan Channar alias M Sivadasan  
   5 C K Gita alias Gita Ravindran"

2. "Resolved that the trustees can nominate only one of his descendants/legal heirs as beneficiary."

3. "Resolved that there will be only one vote each for the above 5(five) members at a time for the trust ie) each trustee will have only one vote."

4. "Resolved further that only one of the legal heirs of the above mentioned five trustees shall have right to become trustee of the trust at a time, so that there will be only 5 trustees always with voting power."

5. "Resolved that any and all of the original authors /trustees of the trust(above 5 trustees) shall have the right to nominate any one of the legal heirs of his or their brother/brothers for the trusteeship of the trust in his place instead of his own direct legal heirs."

6. "Resolved that majority decisions of the trustees will be legally binding in all the matters regarding Alummoottil Tharavad trust."

7. "Resolved that Sri. Ramesh chandran Channar will be the Managing Trustee for the next term ie) three years."

8. "Resolved to open a bank account with Federal Bank - Muttom Branch in the name of Alummoottil Tharavad Trust. There will be three signatories for the operation of Bank accounts. The signing authority will be the Managing Trustee jointly with any one of the other two trustees nominated as signatory. At present M Ramesh chandran, Udayabhanu M and C K Gita."
