## Methodological discipline (errors corrected through this conversation — do not repeat)

- **Quote verbatim or flag as inference.** "R2 swore X" is factual; "R2 committed perjury" is the court's conclusion.
- **"I have not seen X" ≠ "X does not exist"** — never claim absence from silence (D2's WS, the 4 unread resolutions, the Minutes Book contents, etc.).
- **Don't dignify void documents** (2022/2024 Amendments, Nomination, Maradu registration) by arguing from their content — object on the concept.
- **Don't gather confirming evidence** when defendants have admitted (Section 58 IEA) — risks surfacing disconfirming facts. Don't pull SRO Cheppad encumbrance certificate, don't get certified copy from Maradu, don't pull MCA filings unless Section 88 self-dealing argument needs them.
- **Don't threaten Section 215 BNSS reference in interlocutory filings** — reserve for the dedicated application.
- **No numerical scoring** of litigation position (earlier "72/100" withdrawn).
- **Beneficiary status row is CUT from the contradictions matrix** — belongs in plaint/submissions, not the matrix.
- **Trust Deed amendments are VOID — don't reason from their text as operative** (only as admissions against interest, e.g., Clause 17(c) of 2022 Amendment admits beneficiary consent required for leasing).
- **The user has explicitly confirmed:** Radhakrishnan alive, hostile, future defendant — not witness/co-plaintiff in OS 214/2025.
