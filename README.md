## What the suit is

O.S. 214/2025 is a suit filed by **Anoop R. Madhavan** in the **Munsiff Court, Haripad** on **12 June 2025**, asserting his status as a beneficiary of the Alumoottil Tharavad Trust and seeking, in substance, a declaration of that beneficiary status, production of the Trust's accounts and records, and protection of the Trust property against the trustees' dealings. Three interlocutory applications drive the present stage — **IA 2, 5 and 9 of 2025** — with later applications IA 5/2026 and IA 6/2026 added.

## Parties

- **Plaintiff:** Anoop R. Madhavan (USA-resident; through Power of Attorney holder A.G. Kishore, Chennai).
- **D1:** Alumoottil Tharavad Trust (Reg. No. 2/IV/2007, SRO Cheppad, 04/01/2007).
- **D2:** Ramesh Chandran M. — Managing Trustee.
- **D3:** Sivadasan — Author/beneficiary, US citizen holding an OCI card. **Dismissed from this suit on 21/01/2026 for non-service** (restoration not automatic).
- **D4:** Jiji Ramesh — D2's wife.
- **D5–D7:** Arun B. Unni, V. Nandakumar, B. Nadarajan — the three alleged enforcers.

## Court and current status

- **Court:** Munsiff Court, Haripad — Smt. Maneesha K. Bhadran.
- **Next listing:** **28/05/2026**, "for objection and hearing" of IAs 2, 5, 9 of 2025 (substantive hearing; pleadings closed 04/03/2026).
- **Counsel:** HC matters — Adv. Unnikrishnan V. Alapatt. Munsiff — new counsel (Thaha) on record from 18/05/2026.

## Family context

The Plaintiff is the grandson of Alumoottil Chellamma Channatty through her fourth son **Radhakrishnan** (D2's brother and the Plaintiff's father). Radhakrishnan is **alive but hostile to the Plaintiff** and is a contemplated future defendant in a separate breach-of-trust proceeding — he is **not** a witness or co-plaintiff here. The Plaintiff suffered a torn rotator cuff requiring surgery in the **09/08/2024 assault**; **CMP 2828/2024 is already pending** against D2 and D3 for orchestrating it, and **FIR 956/2024** was filed by the Plaintiff against the enforcers.
