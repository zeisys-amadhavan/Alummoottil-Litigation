**Objective 3 — Shut down Sreemoolavasam with maximum financial loss to defendants.** Void the 27/10/2025 lease on three independent grounds: Section 28/49 Registration Act (Maradu registration for Cheppad property); Section 52 TP Act (executed during pendency); Section 11 ITA / Clause 17(c) of the 2022 Amendment (no beneficiary consent). Collateral: complaints to ROC Kochi (Sections 184/188 Companies Act — related party transactions, undisclosed director interests) and GST authorities (invalidation of GSTIN 32ABQCS9416G1Z1 if the principal place of business is occupied without right).

The three objectives reinforce each other: criminal pressure (Obj 1) drives the settlement (Obj 2); voiding the lease (Obj 3) eliminates the defendants' ongoing economic interest in resisting; the consolidated record across all three tracks supports each filing.
