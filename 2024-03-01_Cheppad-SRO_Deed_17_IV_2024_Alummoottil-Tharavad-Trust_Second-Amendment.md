## Operative Clauses

The following clauses were added to the Trust Deed:

**1) New Clause 18:** The name of the Trust shall stand changed from "ALUMOOTTIL THARAVAD TRUST" to **"ALUMMOOTTIL THARAVAD TRUST."**

**2) Clause 19:** This Trust shall be a Non-Discretionary or Specific Private Family Trust.

**3) Clause 20:** The primary area of operation shall be within the territorial jurisdiction of Kerala State, but shall also include other places within or outside the territorial jurisdiction of India.

**4) Clause 21:** The Registered Office of the Trust shall be at Alummoottil House, Haripad, Valyakuzhy Muri, Cheppad Village, Karthikappally Taluk, Alappuzha District, Muttom P.O., PIN – 690511, within the territorial jurisdiction of the Sub Registrar of Cheppad. The Board of Trustees may change the registered office to anywhere else in India by unanimous decision.

**5) Clause 22 — Objectives of the Trust:**
- (a) To conduct smooth and hassle-free administration and management of the properties of the Trust.
- (b) To apply the income and properties of the Trust for the fulfilment of its objects and to distribute the income and properties amongst the Beneficiaries by way of beneficiary share.
- (c) To do all such other lawful acts, deeds and things as are incidental, conducive and necessary to the attainment of the above objectives.

**6) Clause 23 — Board of Trustees:** There shall be a Board of Trustees, which shall be the governing body or Council of the Trust; all Trustees shall be members. The total number of members shall be five. There shall be no specific period of office of Trustees. The Board has power to nominate, elect, disqualify or remove Trustees by unanimous or majority decision, subject to the Trust Deed as amended. Only legal heirs / legatees / testamentary beneficiaries of the Authors and Trustees, and Beneficiaries of the Trust, shall be eligible for Trusteeship.

**7) Clause 24 — Corpus:** The Corpus shall be an amount of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees One Thousand only), vesting in the Trustees in trust for the benefit of the Beneficiaries.

**8) Clause 25 — Current members of the Board of Trustees:**
1. Madhavan Ramesh Chandran Channar alias Ramesh Chandran M. (current **Managing Trustee**)
2. Radhakrishnan M. alias Radhakrishnan Channar
3. M. Udayabhanu alias Udayabhanu Channar
4. Sivadasan A. Madhavan alias Alummoottil Madhavan Sivadasan Channar
5. C.K. Gita

**9) Clause 26:** Madhavan Ramesh Chandran Channar alias Ramesh Chandran M. shall be the Managing Trustee for a further period of three years from the date of execution of this Second Amendment Deed.

**10) Clause 27:** Each Author shall have the right to be represented as / by one Trustee in the Board at all times. Each Author may become a Trustee himself or nominate an eligible person in his place; each nominee Trustee shall in turn have the right to nominate a successor. Each Author, by himself or through his nominee (and so on), shall have one vote in the Board at all times.

**11) Clause 28:** Members of the Board may nominate, by their testaments or otherwise by written nomination letters, their nominees to replace them. Testamentary nominations take effect after demise; non-testamentary nominations take effect during the Trustee's lifetime. Such nominees become Trustees only upon approval of the Board by unanimous or majority decision. On death, resignation, incapacity, disqualification or termination of any Trustee, the Board may nominate (unanimous) or elect (simple majority) a replacement from amongst those eligible.

**12) Clause 29:** Majority decisions of the Board of Trustees shall be legally binding in all matters regarding the Alummoottil Tharavad Trust unless expressly provided otherwise.

**13) Clause 30:** Any and all Authors and trustees shall have the right to nominate anyone from amongst the legal heirs of the said (1) Madhavan Ramesh Chandran Channar alias Ramesh Chandran M., (2) Radhakrishnan M. alias Radhakrishnan Channar, (3) M. Udayabhanu alias Udayabhanu Channar, (4) Sivadasan A. Madhavan alias Alummoottil Madhavan Sivadasan Channar and (5) late Dr. Madhavan Ravindran Channar, for the Trusteeship in his or her place instead of his or her own direct eligible legal heirs.

**14) Clause 31 — Cessation of office:** A Trustee shall cease to hold office:
- (a) if the trustee dies;
- (b) if the trustee resigns;
- (c) if the trustee is adjudged insolvent or found guilty of an offence involving moral turpitude;
- (d) if the trustee is found to have acted against the interest of the Trust;
- (e) if it is unanimously decided by the Board that his/her continuation is against the interest of the Trust.

**15) Clause 32 — Powers, functions and duties of the Trustees:**
- (a) All property of the Trust (movable/immovable, tangible/intangible) shall vest in trust in the Board of Trustees, which shall stand possessed of all such properties acquired by purchase, exchange, grant, allotment, subscription, endowment, donation, contribution or otherwise, and shall manage the whole properties and affairs of the Trust.
- (b) In particular, and without prejudice to the generality of the foregoing, the Board shall have the following powers, duties and functions:
  - (i) To acquire by gift, grant, purchase, exchange, lease or otherwise, lands, buildings or other immovable property, and any movable property.
  - (ii) To construct and maintain buildings, to alter, demolish or improve and equip them suitably; to accept gifts, donations, endowments and contributions (treated as income of the Trust).
  - (iii) To raise loans, receive monies, securities or other movable property on behalf of the Trust.
  - (iv) To accept any trust, trust fund or endowment so long as its provisions are in consonance with the objectives of this Trust.
  - (v) To enter into contracts or engagements on behalf of the Trust.
  - (vi) To consider proposals submitted by Sub-committees and allocate funds as necessary.
  - (vii) To make, sign and execute all such documents and instruments as necessary for management.
  - (viii) To invest monies and funds of the Trust and to vary the investment as deemed fit.
  - (ix) To lease, mortgage or otherwise transfer any interest other than ownership right in immovable property, provided all members of the Board unanimously resolve it is in the interest of the Trust; and to sell/transfer/lease/mortgage/dispose of any movable properties by unanimous resolution.
  - (x) To sell, transfer or otherwise dispose of the **ownership right** in any immovable property only if all members of the Board **as well as all the beneficiaries** resolve it is in the interest of the Trust.
  - (xi) To appoint Sub-Committees/Committees of management with specified powers (advisory/managerial only; all important decisions reserved to the Board).
  - (xii) To appoint employees on such terms as the Trustees deem fit, including power of suspension, dismissal and removal.
  - (xiii) To delegate to the Managing Trustee or any appointed committee such powers, duties and functions.
  - (xiv) To frame Bye-Laws and other regulations.
  - (xv) To appoint new Trustees, or increase/reduce the number or quorum of the Board, and to amend/alter/modify the Bye-Laws, on unanimous decision.
  - (xvi) To maintain a true and correct record of receipts, payments, incomes, expenses and transactions; Books of Account to be maintained by the Managing Trustee and audited by a qualified Chartered Accountant every financial year (1 April – 31 March).
  - (xvii) Out of the income of the Trust property, the Board may spend on: (a) rates, taxes, cess, assessments and dues payable to government/public bodies; (b) insurance premiums for buildings/insurable property; (c) ordinary repairs and amenities; (d) additions/alterations/improvements to buildings; (e) wages and salaries of managers, supervisors, accountants, clerks, servants or other employees; (f) keeping trust property in good condition; (g) installing and renovating electrical and other installations; (h) Architect's / Chartered Accountant's / Advocate's and other professionals' fees; (i) all other costs incidental to management and administration; (j) after deducting such costs, the net income to be distributed amongst beneficiaries in accordance with their beneficiary share.

**16) Clause 33 — Powers of the Managing Trustee:**
- (a) Preside over all meetings of the Board.
- (b) Endorse all Resolutions by signing in affirmation in the Minutes Book.
- (c) Manage day-to-day administration under the guidance, control and supervision of the Board.
- (d) Convene meetings of the Board at least once every year.
- (e) Circulate notices and agenda; keep records of minutes; maintain the Minutes Book; certify Certified True Copies of Resolutions.
- (f) Record proceedings of all meetings and put them up for approval at the next meeting.
- (g) Execute, enforce and carry out the directives of the Board; be responsible for implementation of decisions; conduct ordinary business and correspondence.
- (h) Be responsible for updating and maintaining the list of members of the Board.
- (i) Exercise all powers required for the proper conduct of ordinary business; keep accurate lists and accounts of properties and assets; receive donations and render accounts periodically.
- (j) Maintain a Book of Accounts, get it audited by a qualified Chartered Accountant within two months of the close of the financial year, and submit it for the Board's approval.
- (k) Keep in safe custody all valuable documents relating to the Trust.
- (l) Execute all deeds, agreements, leases, releases, mortgages, gifts, settlements and other documents for and on behalf of the Board.
- (m) Have power to organise an Annual family meet or get-together of the Trustees and Beneficiaries and their spouses and children whenever possible.

**17) Clause 34:** In the event of death, resignation, incapacity, disqualification or termination of the Managing Trustee, the Board shall have power to nominate (unanimous) or elect (simple majority) a new Managing Trustee from amongst those eligible, during the period of office of such Board, subject to the condition that such Managing Trustee shall be a member of the concerned Board of Trustees.

**18) Clause 35:** All Authors and Trustees of the Trust shall be Beneficiaries of the Trust as well.

**19) Clause 36 — Distribution of income:**
- (a) All income shall be divided into five equal shares and distributed amongst the Authors (in their capacity as Beneficiaries), one share to each Author.
- (b) On death of any Author, that share shall go to the legatees / testamentary beneficiaries in accordance with The Indian Succession Act, 1925 (testamentary succession).
- (c) On death of any Author, that share shall go to the legal heirs in accordance with The Hindu Succession Act, 1956 (intestate succession).

**20) Clause 37 — Rules for meetings and proceedings of the Board:**
- (a) Trustees shall ordinarily meet at least once a financial year, or as often as necessary.
- (b) A Minutes Book shall be kept by the Managing Trustee.
- (c) 15 (Fifteen) days' prior written Notice shall ordinarily be given by the Managing Trustee for a meeting. Emergency meetings may be convened on 3 (three) days' prior notice. The Quorum shall be a minimum of 3 (three) members. Decisions may be made at a meeting or by circulation of papers (routine matters); important matters only at a meeting. All matters shall be decided by simple majority of those present; in the event of a tie, the Managing Trustee shall have a casting vote in addition to his/her own vote.

**21) Clause 38 — Bank Accounts:** The Board may open Bank Account(s) in any Nationalised, Scheduled, Commercial, Co-operative Bank, Financial Institution or Co-operative Society in the name of **Alummoottil Tharavad Trust**, operated by the Managing Trustee. Accounts may also be opened in the name of institutions/organisations founded by the Trust.

**22) Clause 39:** The income, properties and funds shall be solely used towards the benefit of the beneficiaries in accordance with their beneficiary share and for due administration; after deducting costs, the net income shall be distributed amongst beneficiaries (subject to Board approval). This shall not preclude reimbursement of expenses, remuneration, allowance, residential accommodation or perquisites to any member of the Board, employee or sub-committee member in connection with work carried out for the Trust, subject to Board approval.

**23) Clause 40 — Records to be kept:**
- (a) Register of Members of the Board and of Committees/Sub-Committees;
- (b) Attendance Register;
- (c) Minutes Book;
- (d) Accounts Books;
- (e) Receipts and Vouchers, etc.;
- (f) Register for valuables;
- (g) Register for furniture and other movable properties;
- (h) Register for immovable properties;
- (i) File of documents related to movable properties;
- (j) File of documents related to immovable properties;
- (k) File of Trust Deed and its Amendment Deeds.

**24) Clause 41:** No act of Trustees shall be invalidated by reason only of any vacancy in the Board or any irregularity in the proceedings, subject to ratification by the Board.

**25) Clause 42:** Amendments to the Trust Deed shall be made only by 2/3 (two-third) majority or more amongst the members of the Board.

**26) Clause 43:** In case of failure of the Trust for want of its objectives, or the Trust becoming impossible/dysfunctional, the Board shall have power to revoke the Trust, only by unanimous decision of the Board and the beneficiaries, by special resolution. On revocation, the assets remaining after deducting all debts and liabilities shall be distributed among the beneficiaries in accordance with their beneficiary share.

**27)** This Second Amendment Deed shall have **overriding effect** over the Resolutions taken by the Board at their meeting held on 22/12/2023 (erroneously written as 22/11/2023 in the Minutes Book by clerical error) wherever they conflict; where they do not conflict, the Resolutions of 22/12/2023 shall remain in effect.

**28)** The clauses in the Trust Deed as amended by the First Amendment Deed, as further amended by this Second Amendment Deed, shall continue to exist and hold good; wherever amended by this Second Amendment Deed, such amendments shall have overriding effect.

**29)** Subject to the amendments made hereinabove, all provisions of the Trust Deed as amended by the First Amendment Deed shall remain in force.

**30)** A Stamp duty of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees One Thousand only) is paid for this document as per Article 57.a of the Schedule of The Kerala Stamp Act, 1959.
