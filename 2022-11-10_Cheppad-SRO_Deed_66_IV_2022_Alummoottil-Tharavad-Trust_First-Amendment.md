## Operative Clauses

Now this Deed witnesses as follows:

**1) The following Clauses are to be added to the said Trust Deed as Clause No. 17:**

- **(a)** The trustees and their brothers are the original executors of the Trust. The original Authors of the Trust and their legal heirs are the beneficiaries of the Trust.
- **(b)** The beneficiaries shall have no rights to sell or transfer their shares to anyone outside of the legal heirs of the original five Trustees.
- **(c)** Transfer of Title of Trust properties and/or leasing out the Trust properties — moveable or immovable or tangible or intangible — can only be done **upon consent of all Trustees and beneficiaries.**
- **(d)** Spouses, if and when they cease to be husbands or wives of the concerned Authors and beneficiaries, shall cease to have the right to be the trustees and/or the beneficiaries of the Trust, except when they are custodians of minor legal heirs or incapacitated children of the Authors and beneficiaries of the Trust.
- **(e)** Only the Authors and/or the Beneficiaries of the Trust shall have the right to hold the post of the Trustees of the Trust.
- **(f)** A parental custodian or a natural guardian or a legal custodian or guardian by court order of any minor or incapacitated beneficiary can act as a proxy holder of Trusteeship for the concerned time period.
- **(g)** This Trust shall be a Private Trust.

**2) Clause No. 15 of the Trust Deed shall be modified as given below:**

On death of any of the original Trustees of the Trust, the Board of Trustees, by nomination or majority decision, shall have the power to appoint new trustees, provided such new trustees shall be legal heirs of the original five Authors of the Trust. Spouses as custodians of their minor children, or children who are not capable of making decisions by themselves, or as holders of legally valid proxy of their natural born adult legal heirs, shall also be eligible for trusteeship for the concerned time period.

**3)** The clauses in the said Trust Deed, except as modified by this Deed of Amendment of Trust Deed, shall continue to exist and hold good, and the Authors of the Trust and Trustees shall be liable to adhere to the same. Wherever the clauses of the said Trust Deed are amended vide this Deed of Amendment of Trust Deed, such amendments shall have overriding effect over the clauses of the said Trust Deed.

**4)** Subject to the amendments made hereinabove, all the provisions of the Trust Deed registered as No. 2/2007 of Cheppad Sub Registry, executed on 04-01-2007, shall remain in force.

A Stamp duty of Rs. 1,000/- (Rupees One Thousand only) is paid for this document.
