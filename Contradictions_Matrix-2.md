R2's own description in IA 18/05/2026 Para 3 of that Sub-Registrar's Office as being in *"Alappuzha"* is itself wrong — Maradu is in Ernakulam, not Alappuzha. None of R2's three filings asserts any office, property, or other connection of the Trust to Ernakulam District. Section 28 of the Registration Act, 1908 is mandatory: a document affecting immovable property must be registered at the SRO within whose sub-district the property is situated. The test is the situs of the property, not the residence of parties, the office of the lessee, or the convenience of any party — none of which can override Section 28. The Sub-Registrar at Maradu had no jurisdiction to register a lease over Cheppad property; his act of registration was *ultra vires* his own authority. The lease is consequently void and inadmissible in evidence under Section 49. |
