# BEFORE THE HONOURABLE MUNSIFF COURT, HARIPAD

## OS NO. 214/2025

*Objections filed by Respondents 5, 6 and 7 in IA.2/2025, IA.9/2025 and IA.5/2025*

> **Note:** The three objections below are substantially identical in text, differing only in the interlocutory application (IA) number. Each is reproduced in full as filed.

## IA. 2/2025

| | |
|---|---|
| **Petitioner** | Anoop R Madhavan |
| **Respondents** | Alummoottil Trust and 6 others |

**Objection filed by the respondents 5, 6, 7 in the above petitions.**

1. The petition is false, vexatious and not maintainable against these respondents.

2. 5th respondent is the temporary site supervisor deputed by the contractor. Respondent No. 6 is a secretary of Sree Rama Krishna Asram managed by SNDP branch, Muttom and 7th respondent is president of Sree Rama Krishna Asram managed by SNDP branch, Muttom and it is closely connected with Alummoottil Tharavad.

3. These respondent's 5, 6, 7 have no connection with Alummoottil Tharavadu Trust. They never interfered in the affairs of the trust and it administration. 5th respondent is not having direct connection with the trust or trustees. 5th respondent was the worker of the contractor who had taken work for the 3rd respondent.

4. These respondent's are fully ignorant regarding the affairs of the trust, trust deed, beneficiary. So the allegation regarding the affairs of the trust and trust deed described in affidavit are to be proved by the petitioner. These facts are to be adjudicated between petitioner and respondent 1 to 3.

5. There respondent's are totally unnecessary parties in the above petition.

6. Petitioner is not entitled to get any relief against 5, 6 and 7 respondent's.

Hence it is prayed that this court may be pleased to dismiss the petition with the cost of 5, 6 and 7th respondent's.

Dated this the 18th day of February 2026.
