## Affidavit

*Before the Hon'ble Munsiff Court, Haripad — IA No. ___ of 2025, In OS No. 214 of 2025.*

*Anoop R. Madhavan ... Plaintiff*
*Vs.*
*Alumoottil Tharavadu Trust & Ors. ... Defendants*

I, Anoop Radhakrishnan Madhavan, aged 48 years, S/o Radhakrishnan Madhavan, residing at 1201 Connecticut Ave NW Ste 531, PMB # 722189, Washington DC, USA, represented by my duly constituted Agent holding Power of Attorney, AG Kishore, aged 71 years, S/o AK Gopalan, r/o 12 TV Road, Chetpet, Chennai, Tamil Nadu – 600031, do hereby sincerely and solemnly affirm and state as follows:

1. I am the plaintiff in the above suit and I am conversant with the facts and hence competent to swear this affidavit.

2. The above suit is filed for a declaration that the Plaintiff is a Beneficiary under Trust Deed No. 2/IV/2007 of the Alummoottil Tharavad Trust and for consequential reliefs including a declaration that Amendment Deeds No. 66/IV/2022 and 17/IV/2024 are illegal and void. The suit further seeks directions to restore the Plaintiff's rights over the ancestral Meda property, to restrain the Defendants from committing further acts of exclusion and to compel the production of the Trust accounts. The suit is filed based on the Plaintiff's lawful rights as a lineal descendant and Co-Beneficiary under the original Trust Deed No. 2/IV/2007 dated 04/01/2007.

3. The Alummoottil Tharavad Trust was constituted by Trust Deed No. 2/IV/2007, dated 04-01-2007, registered at the Sub-Registrar's Office, Cheppad. Page 3, paragraph 5 states that the Trust is established for the benefit of the "descendants of Alummoottil Chellamma Channatty."

4. The Hon'ble High Court of Kerala, by order dated ……… in ………, (Exhibit A) has granted me a certified copy of the Trust Deed of Alummoottil Tharavad Trust, being document No. 2/IV/2007 (the certified copy is produced herewith as Exhibit B).

5. The Managing Trustee of the Trust (Defendant No. 2) has not produced a single statement of accounts to me as a beneficiary for the last eighteen years, i.e., since the formation of the Trust in 2007. This is despite several verbal requests and three written requests and despite the duties under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882, including Sections 19 and 57, which recognise the beneficiary's right to receive and inspect accounts.

6. In O.S. No. 214/2025, I have specifically pleaded inter alia in I.A. No. 9/2025, that several family heirlooms, including wildlife artefacts are missing from Alummoottil Meda. In O.S. No. 84/2024, I have complained that the trustees are blocking my access to my ancestral home. In O.S. No. 243/2024, they have sought to mislead the court about my rights in the Trust. In these circumstances, their assurances cannot safely be taken at face value.

7. In this suit, I.A. No. 2/2025 has been filed for production of trust documents and I.A. No. 5/2025 has been filed for production of accounts. I.A. No. 9/2025 concerns the missing heirlooms and wildlife artefacts. All these interlocutory applications are pending consideration before this Hon'ble Court.

8. The trustees are now proposing to start a commercial business venture at Alummoottil Meda, with an inauguration around 20.12.2025, by permitting participation of members of the public.

9. If the proposed commercial venture is allowed to commence before I.A. No. 2/2025 (documents), I.A. No. 5/2025 (accounts) and I.A. No. 9/2025 (heirlooms/wildlife artefacts) are heard and fully complied with, financial and inventory accountability for the period up to the commencement of the venture will effectively be lost once the public begins to enter and use the property.

10. I humbly submit that, in these circumstances, the balance of convenience is entirely in my favour as plaintiff beneficiary. Permitting the venture to start now will cause irreparable loss of accountability in respect of the trust properties and finances for the period prior to commencement of the new venture, which cannot later be cured by damages.

11. It is therefore most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to

    a. Direct the respondent trustees to maintain strict status quo in respect of Alummoottil Tharavad Trust property and restrain them from commencing any commercial venture there, until I.A. No. 2/2025, I.A. No. 5/2025 and I.A. No. 9/2025 in O.S. 214/2025 are heard and disposed of.

    b. Direct the respondent trustees not to conduct any events, inaugurations or activities in Alummoottil Tharavad Trust property by virtue of which any non-trust participants, other than the Authors, Trustees and Beneficiaries, enter the premises for any function or event, normal day-to-day labourers alone being exempted

    c. Treat this application as urgent in view of the proposed inauguration around 20.12.2025 and

12. A separate petition for the said purpose is filed along with and if the same is not allowed I will be put to irreparable injury and hardships.

Dated this the 11th day of December 2025

All the facts stated above are true and correct to the best of my knowledge, information and belief.

*Deponent*

Solemnly affirmed and signed before me by the deponent on this the 31st day of March, 2026 in my office at Ernakulam.

*Dheeraj Krishnan Perot, Advocate*
